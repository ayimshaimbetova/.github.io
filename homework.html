Alatoo International University - Gateway to the world

Ala-Too International University (AIU) was established in 1996 and it is located in Bishkek, the Kyrgyz Republic. Our university consists of five blocks, where there are classrooms and laboratories equipped with the Internet and modern interactive whiteboards. Our "Campus" is an educational center with a library, a conference hall, 3 stadiums, 1 tennis cord, 1 gym, 6 computer classes, 1 Internet club, 1 research center, 2 cantinas, 1 medical center.

There are 4 faculties, 3 institutes and 16 departments in AIU. The faculties are:

  • Faculty of Engineering and Informatics
  • Faculty of Social Sciences
  • Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences
  • Faculty of Medicine

The staffing of the AIU is composed of highly qualified, experienced, young, active teachers from Kyrgyzstan, Turkey, USA, China, India and other countries. Undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate programs are conducted in a multicultural and democratic atmosphere of mutual respect. Our university has bilateral agreements with some universities in Europe, China, America, Turkey and Cyprus.

About us
The Ministry of Education and Science of the Kyrgyz Republic is the State governing body. The founder of Ala-Too University is the Sapat International Educational Institution. Ala-Too International University (MUA) was established in 1996 and is located in Bishkek, Kyrgyz Republic. AMU is a legal entity, receives its activities in accordance with the application of the Kyrgyz Republic. The governing bodies are: - the highest body - the Founder; - executive body - Rector - Sanjarbek Erdolatov; - collegial body - academic council of the university.

Language
The main language of instruction is English. Russian, Kyrgyz and Turkish are auxiliary languages. In addition, students of individual faculties can choose a second foreign language, Japanese, German, Chinese, French are provided for their choice. For those who do not speak English, there are preparatory courses. Upon graduation from the university, a state-recognized diploma of higher professional education of the Kyrgyz Republic is issued
